# Eldoria Chronicles

A text-based fantasy RPG built in Python with a deep branching story, turn-based combat, and multiple endings.

## Story

One hundred years ago, the sorcerer Malachar shattered the five Crystal Shards that kept Eldoria alive. A prophecy survives: *'One soul from the dust shall rise — and either save the world, or become its end.'* That soul is you.

## Features

- **5-Act story** with branching dialogue and meaningful choices
- **4 character classes** — Knight of Ashenveil, Shadowblade, Arcanist, Warden of the Wild
- **Turn-based combat** with skills, items, and enemy AI
- **Levelling system** with XP and stat growth
- **Inventory & items** — Health Potions, Ether Vials, Smoke Bombs
- **3 unique endings** depending on your choices throughout the game
- **Moral complexity** — the villain may not be what he seems

## How to Play

```bash
python3 eldoria_chronicles.py
```

No dependencies required beyond the Python standard library.

## The Endings

- **The Warrior's Crown** — fight to the end and claim victory through strength
- **The Price of Mercy** — give a broken man the chance to redeem himself
- **The Unlikely Alliance** — trust where trust seems impossible

*The choice is yours.*
